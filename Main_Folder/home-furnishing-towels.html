Enhance your bathing experience with Monte Carlos towels.

Monte Carlo brings to you a range of exquisite hand and bath towels that will not only add to your bathing experience but also look great on that stand in your bathroom. Made from cotton, these towels absorb water well and do not feel abrasive on the skin. There is a wide range of colors available - from earthy browns and beiges to cool blues and greens to warm reds and yellows. You can choose between the lighter 525 GSM towels and the heavier 625 GSM towels.

Monte Carlo towels are made on state-of-the-art machinery under the supervision of our designers and expert craftsmen. Each towel is inspected thoroughly for quality before it is shipped to our customers.

You can easily purchase Monte Carlos towels online. Simply add the selected towels to your online shopping cart. Provide your details such as address and payment to complete your purchase. We deliver to most pincodes across India, and have prepaid and COD payment options. Your order will be delivered to you right at your doorstep within a few days. Happy shopping!
